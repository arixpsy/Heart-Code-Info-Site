Problem Statement

Many deaf people face the possibility of accidents because there are limitations to awareness of their surroundings without or with limited hearing

Prone to Injuries

Injuries can be from mild to severe. This danger is not only limited to themselves but those around them

Insecurities

Insecurities can come when there is a possibility of theft around isolated areas

PRODUCT

GotYourBack device is a backpack that features multiple sensors that will alert users of incoming objects behind them.

Led & vibrating motor

The led will blink when an incoming object is approaching behind user.
Types of Blinking Mode:

    Solid (When object is not moving at all
    Slow blinking (when object is approaching slowly)
    Fast blinking (when object is fast approaching)

Range Sensors

The sensor will detect the range of an incoming object behind the users and send the data towards the Indicators and notify the users

Portability

The compact design of he product allows it to be placed in almost any carrier,therefore eliminating the limitations of a single design or carrier
