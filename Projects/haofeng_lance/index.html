All about the digital senses

The digital senses are bluetooth devices which are connected to one another in pairs. once it is out of range, the devices will vibrate accordding to how far it is and inform the user about it. This allows the visually impaired to know that his or her caretake had left, thus asking for help. Easy to setup, simple to use!

Features of the Digital Senses

Vibrates outside range

Alerts you when you are far away from the visually impaired.

Small, Light & Portable

A small handheld microbit for your partner to never lose track of them.

Always connected

Up to a distance of 15m, the Digital Senses will alert you according to the distance.

Emergency Light

An emergency feature to notify you when the visually impaired falls.
