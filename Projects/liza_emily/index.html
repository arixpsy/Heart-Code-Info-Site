You gotta hand it to the
visually impaired

The Singapore Association of the Visually Handicapped (SAVH) has more than 4,000 members and the prevalence rate of the visually impaired is 63 per 10,000 population. One of their struggles would be doing daily activities independently such as taking their medication.

Our Solution

Our Solution

How can we help the visually impaired to
take their medication independently?

Vibration reminder

A wearable device that will remind the visually impaired to eat their medicine by vibrating when it is time to take the medication.

Locate Medication

When you're in the room with the medication, the wearable will make a sound for the visually impaired to find their medication.
