What does Angel Eye do?

Being unable to hear makes the hearing impaired constantly wary of anyone wanting to interact with them as the lack of hearing makes it impossible for them to tell when someone is approaching behind them or when someone is trying to get their attention.

The Angel Eye scrutinises the environment around the user for persons and informs them when someone is near them. This helps to relieve the hearing impaired when out in public as they will know if anyone is approaching them or trying to interact with them.

The sensor attached to the Eye detects people in a small area around the user and lights up an LED and sends a vibration to alert the user.The closer the person is to the user, the faster the LED will blink, and increased intensity of the vibration.



What is Project Heartcode?

Project Heartcode is Singapore Management University School of Information System's community service project that exposes youth to HTML, CSS and JQuery and gives them an opportunity to come up with a product that makes the lives of the less fortunate easier.
